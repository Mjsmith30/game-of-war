UPPER HAND

Instructions

Each Player turns up a card by clicking "Fight" and the player with the higher card takes both. Start by clicking fight, which will shuffle and deal 26 cards to each player. In order to win one player must collect 30 cards or not fall below a total of 22 cards. If the cards are the same rank each player will continue to draw until a higher card is drawn. The player with higher card will be the winner and take both piles to win the game!
